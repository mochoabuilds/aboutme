Michael Valentino Ochoa is a microeconomist who previously worked as a cook, publicist and software designer as their creative medium.

Born in Chicago, Michael's work has been recognized by the University of Illinois Urbana Champaign and Bradley University.

He is also known for building community and has become a go-to person for economics and art history, as well as for those interested in engaging new audiences through economics-driven storytelling.

As of 2026 he is a graduate student in Economics. Samples of his academic work include Health Economics, Law & Economics, and TBD Developmental Economics. He also worked weekends at Buttercup during that time.

Michael is based in Chicago and aims to visit 3 neighborhoods each day.
